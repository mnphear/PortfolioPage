About

PROFILE 2026

I work where the technical meets the made-up. A background in compositing taught me to respect the plate — colour, grain, the physics of light — and that discipline runs through everything else I touch.

I move between VFX, concept art, sculpture and real-time work without treating them as separate worlds. The pipeline is one continuous thing, and I'm happy living at any point along it — from a Nuke node graph to a Blender geometry tree to a Unity shader.

Based in Ljubljana, working independently and open to collaboration.
